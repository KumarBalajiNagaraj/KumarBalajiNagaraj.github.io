Next few days, a notification - Naveen wants to connect.

That yes made our way to Linkedin - actually linked us far beyond professionally and now into more generations!! So yes, love is all about getting linked!!

Events

08th June - Wednesday

Engagement Rituals05:00PM - 10:00PM

This is the starting event in the entire wedding ceremony. Inviting the Groom, Welcoming everyone and Nichayathartham takes place in this event. Also at Nichayathartham ceremony, the bride and groom exchange rings to confirm their start of the life long blessed journey.

09th June - Thursday

Wedding 07:30AM - 9AM

This is the main event in the entire wedding when the bride and the groom tie the knot. The groom comes with the barat and is greeted by the bride side with some dhol of course as maximum number of guests would be attending this event. This is when the couple circle the sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.

Wedding Feast12PM - 2PM

Tamilnadu holds significant traditions in weddings, and a traditional Tamil Cuisine is characterized by its dishes whose origins are centuries ago. The conventional way of serving the food in banana leaf in a subtle style and a good ambience. The blend of mustard seed, garlic, ginger, chilli, pepper, cloves, and nutmeg. Traditionally sweets are served ahead of the other dishes, and then the meal and the main courses are served.

15th June - Wednesday

Reception 06PM-09PM

Once all the ceremonies and rituals are finished, a grand reception is thrown Here amazing South Indian food is served which is usually non-vegetarian and to tell you the truth if you every attend a South Indian Wedding then the food there is out of the world. Food along with some traditional music total encapsulates the whole environment.

Ugh... dress codes?

Mehndi 1PM - 5PM

Ethnic indian wear
Ladies can wear something bright & vivid and gentlemen can be on their vibrant kurtas with pants or pyjamas along with an optional shawl.

Cocktail Night 7PM - 12AM

Western attire
A semi-formal look that is contemporary & fashionable which is not too prim & proper is the best to pull off this night. You can see some pins for ladies & gents.

Wedding 6PM-12AM

Indo-western
Ladies can flaunt their unconventional sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, dresses and the list goes on whereas gentlemen can wear any kind of suit or just blazer. If you don't like to wear a suit/blazer, you can do a high-neck (band gala) waistcoat & shirt combo.
